Common Refrigerator Issues & Solutions

Problem Our Solution
Refrigerator Not Cooling Diagnose low refrigerant, clean condenser coils, or repair compressor faults to restore cooling.
Strange Noises Fix motor or defrost issues causing buzzing or grinding sounds for quiet operation.
Leaking Water Clear clogged defrost drains or repair water lines to prevent kitchen damage.
Ice Maker Not Working Repair frozen lines or defective valves to restore ice production.
Freezer Not Freezing Address blocked vents, defrost faults, or compressor issues for proper freezing.
Over-Freezing Fix faulty thermostats or damper controls to prevent food spoilage.
Fridge Running Continuously Repair condenser coils or door seals to improve energy efficiency.
Power Issues Diagnose and fix faulty wiring or electrical components safely.
