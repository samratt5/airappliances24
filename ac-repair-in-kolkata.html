Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - AC Services

How often should I service my AC?

It is recommended to service your AC at least twice a year – before summer and after the cooling season ends. Regular maintenance helps improve efficiency and extends the lifespan of your AC unit.

What types of ACs do you repair?

We repair all types of air conditioners, including Window AC, Split AC, Cassette AC, Ductable AC, Central AC, and Commercial HVAC systems.

Do you provide AC gas filling services?

Yes, we offer AC gas refilling for R32, R22, and R410A refrigerants to ensure efficient cooling. Our experts check for gas leaks before refilling.

What are the signs that my AC needs repair?

Common signs include insufficient cooling, water leakage, unusual noises, compressor issues, fan malfunctions, thermostat problems, or electrical faults.

How much does AC repair service cost?

The cost varies depending on the issue. Basic servicing starts at ₹199, while repairs like gas refilling, compressor replacement, and PCB repair have additional charges. A detailed estimate is provided before any work begins.

Do you offer any warranty on services?

Yes, we provide a warranty on spare parts and service to ensure quality and reliability. The duration is clearly mentioned in your service invoice.

How can I book a service?

Booking is simple! Call us or fill out the contact form on our website to schedule a convenient time slot for service.
