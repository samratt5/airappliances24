Common Refrigerator Problems in Kolkata and Proven Solutions

Kolkata's tropical climate, with high humidity and frequent power cuts, often leads to fridge malfunctions. Here's an in-depth guide to the most prevalent issues and how we resolve them swiftly.

  • Not Cooling Properly: Caused by low refrigerant or dirty evaporator coils. Solution: Thorough cleaning and gas refilling.
  • Water Leakage: Due to clogged drains or worn seals, common in monsoon conditions. Solution: On-site drain unclogging and seal replacement.
  • Unusual Noises: Fan blade issues or loose components. Solution: Precision diagnostics with vibration testing tools.
  • Power and Electrical Faults: Frequent in unstable voltage conditions. Solution: Surge protector installation and wiring upgrades.
  • Ice Build-Up: Defrost timer failures. Solution: Timer calibration and heater element checks.
  • Odor and Bacterial Growth: Prevalent in food-heavy environments. Solution: Deep sanitization with antimicrobial agents.
  • Thermostat Malfunctions: Inconsistent temperatures. Solution: Digital thermostat swaps for precise control.
