Why is my refrigerator not cooling properly?

A fridge may stop cooling due to dirty condenser coils, a faulty thermostat, low refrigerant, or compressor issues. If food isn’t staying fresh, it’s time for expert help. Our technicians provide quick and reliable refrigerator repair in Kolkata, ensuring your fridge cools perfectly again.

What should I do if my refrigerator is making loud noises?

Unusual sounds usually come from the fan motor, evaporator, or compressor. Sometimes, leveling the fridge helps reduce noise. If the sound persists, call our expert fridge repair technicians in Kolkata for a quick and reliable fix.

Why is my refrigerator leaking water?

Water leakage often happens due to a clogged defrost drain, faulty inlet valve, or a damaged water tray. While minor blockages can be cleared, frequent leaks require professional help. Our certified refrigerator service in Kolkata solves leakage issues with guaranteed long-term solutions.

Why is my refrigerator freezer not freezing?

A non-freezing freezer can be due to thermostat issues, blocked vents, refrigerant leaks, or compressor faults. Our expert refrigerator repair technicians in Kolkata diagnose the root cause and restore freezing efficiency quickly.
