Fridge Maintenance Tips to Extend Lifespan

Regular maintenance can prevent costly repairs and keep your refrigerator running efficiently. Here are expert tips from Airappliances Kolkata to maintain your fridge:

  • Clean Condenser Coils: Dust and debris on coils can reduce cooling efficiency. Clean them every 6 months using a vacuum or brush.
  • Check Door Seals: Ensure gaskets are tight to prevent air leaks, which can strain the compressor.
  • Defrost Regularly: Avoid ice buildup in the freezer to maintain optimal freezing performance.
  • Monitor Temperature: Set your fridge to 3–5°C and freezer to -18°C for best results and energy savings.
  • Avoid Overloading: Overfilling can block air vents, leading to uneven cooling and spoilage.
  • Schedule Annual Maintenance: Book our ₹499 annual service to keep your fridge in top condition.
