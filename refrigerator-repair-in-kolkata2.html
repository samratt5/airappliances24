Common Refrigerator Problems (Causes & Quick Checks)

Refrigerators are essential household appliances, but they can encounter issues over time. Understanding common problems, their causes, and quick checks can help you decide when to call a professional. Here are the most frequent issues we address at Airappliances Kolkata:

  • Refrigerator Not Cooling
    • Cause: Low refrigerant, dirty condenser coils, or a faulty compressor.
    • Quick Check: Ensure the thermostat is set correctly (3–5°C) and clean the coils if accessible. If the problem persists, contact us.
  • Strange Noises
    • Cause: Worn-out motor, loose components, or defrost issues.
    • Quick Check: Check for loose items inside the fridge and ensure it’s level. Persistent noise requires expert attention.
  • Leaking Water
    • Cause: Clogged defrost drain or damaged water lines.
    • Quick Check: Inspect the drain pan and clear any blockages with warm water. Seek professional help for leaks.
  • Ice Maker Not Working
    • Cause: Frozen lines or defective valves.
    • Quick Check: Ensure the water supply is on and the freezer is cold enough (-18°C). Call for repairs if needed.
  • Freezer Not Freezing
    • Cause: Blocked vents, defrost system failure, or compressor issues.
    • Quick Check: Rearrange items to clear vents and check the temperature setting. Professional diagnosis is recommended.

These quick checks can help identify minor issues, but for complex repairs, our technicians are just a call away at +91 8250791200.

Fridge Maintenance Tips to Extend Lifespan

Regular maintenance can prevent costly repairs and keep your refrigerator running efficiently. Here are expert tips from Airappliances Kolkata to maintain your fridge:

  • Clean Condenser Coils: Dust and debris on coils can reduce cooling efficiency. Clean them every 6 months using a vacuum or brush.
  • Check Door Seals: Ensure gaskets are tight to prevent air leaks, which can strain the compressor.
  • Defrost Regularly: Avoid ice buildup in the freezer to maintain optimal freezing performance.
  • Monitor Temperature: Set your fridge to 3–5°C and freezer to -18°C for best results and energy savings.
  • Avoid Overloading: Overfilling can block air vents, leading to uneven cooling and spoilage.
  • Schedule Annual Maintenance: Book our ₹499.00 annual service to keep your fridge in top condition.
