Fridge Not Cooling in Kolkata's Humid Climate? Causes & Fixes

The system is running but failing to reach temperature. In Kolkata, this often points to gas leakage from the cooling pipe or a faulty evaporator fan in the freezer. We perform a detailed pressure check for leakage and provide R600a/R134a gas refilling on the spot, usually fixing the issue within 3 hours.

Compressor Overheating or Clicking Sound (Not Starting)

If your compressor cycles quickly or fails to start, it's usually a failed start relay/overload protector or a dead digital inverter board. Replacing these small components often solves the issue and prevents the need for a costly compressor replacement. We carry relays for all major brands.

Water Leakage Inside or Outside the Fridge

This is often caused by a clogged defrost drain line or a damaged/loose door seal. Our technician will clean the drain pipe and test the defroster. If the seal is the issue, we carry replacement seals for most single and double-door models for a quick fix.
